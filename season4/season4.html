Season 4

The Legends are ready to take a breather after killing the demon Mallus
by snuggling him to death with a big stuffed bear named Beebo.
Sara and her crew work with Ava Sharpe and the Time Bureau to eliminate the
final few anachronisms. The assignment appears to be simple enough
until Constantine arrives and informs them that by fixing one significant
problem, they have created another, much larger one. When the
Legends allowed time to collapse in order to free and vanquish Mallus,
the barrier between worlds weakened. "Fugitives" - mystical creatures
from mythology, fairy tales, and folklore â€“ have infiltrated history.
These Fugitives, who have been exiled throughout history by heroes such as
Constantine, are again returning to our planet in droves and wreaking havoc.
Because the Time Bureau is wary of magic and ill-equipped to cope with it,
the Legends must join forces with everyone's favourite demonologist to rewrite history.
