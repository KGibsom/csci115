Season 7

The Legends have saved the Earth from an alien invasion in 1925,
so they thought they could go home and relax. Too bad their time travel ship,
the Waverider, was blown up by a second Waverider...
so now they're trapped in 1925 Odessa, Texas! Only way to get back home and
get revenge on their mysterious new enemy is to journey to New York City
and find the inventor of the Waverider, Welsh Dr. Gwyn Davies. But with no Waverider,
no way to know how they're impacting the timeline, and a time traveling foe,
it might be impossible. Along the way, they meet a surprising new teammate:
a human Gideon, whose knowledge of the timeline may provide the answer
to all of the Legends' problems.

Characters

Constantine and Mick leave and in there place comes Gwyn Davies and Human Gideon.
Gideon who was previously the waverider's A.I. is now human.
There's no real main antagonist this season,
but they do meet robot versions of themselves.
