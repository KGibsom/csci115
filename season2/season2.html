Season 2

After the defeat of Vandal Savage and the corrupt Time Masters, a new threat emerges.
Dr. Nate Heywood, a historian, joins the Legends. When the Legends encounter the J.S.A
(the Justice Society of America) in the 1940s, Amaya Jiwe, aka Vixen, joins the team.
While the crew reassembles, a question looms: what happened to former captain Rip Hunter?
The Legends continue their new goal of defending the timeline against temporal aberrations,
which are extraordinary events in history that might have disastrous effects.
Finally, the Legends will face off against both old and new enemies in order to
defend the planet from a strange new menace.

Characters

With Leonard dead, Kendra & Carter gone, and Rip missing, two new members join the Legends.
Nathaniel "Nate" Heywood and Amaya Jiwe join Sara, Ray, Martin, Jefferson, and Mick.
