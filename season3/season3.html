Season 3

Following the destruction of Eobard Thawne and his equally sinister Legion of Doom,
the Legends face a new threat spawned by their actions at the end of the previous season.
By returning to a previous point in time, they have effectively shattered the
chronology and generated anachronisms - a dispersion of people, animals, and
artifacts throughout time! Before the time stream collapses, our team must discover
a means to return all the anachronisms to their rightful timelines. But, before
our Legends can re-enter the fray, Rip Hunter and his newly formed Time Bureau bring
their tactics into doubt. The Legends disband until they are needed again since
the Time Bureau is practically the new sheriffs in town. The Legends will question
the Time Bureau's power over the timeline, claiming that no matter how clumsy their
techniques are, some issues are beyond the Bureau's ability.
Some issues can only be resolved by Legends.
