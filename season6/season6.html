Season 6

After spending the previous season attempting to locate the
Loom of Fate in order to resurrect their murdered friends,
the Legends must now defend history from an adversary unlike
any they've faced in the past or future. They've battled with monsters,
temporal anomalies, and, most recently, former villains,
but this season they'll confront something much more difficult and odd...
space aliens! After one of their own is kidnapped by a violent
extraterrestrial, this new mission takes on new meaning.
It will require more than the Legends' combined abilities.
They must find a new Legend, a lady who was taken by aliens
as a youngster and is said to be endowed with talents that would
aid the squad in defeating their cosmic foes.

Characters

Ray, Nora, Mona, and Charlie all depart.
The Legends recruit a new member with special abilities.
The new foes are out of this world!
