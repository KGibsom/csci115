Season 1

In the year 2166, Time Master Rip Hunter loses his wife and child to the
immortal tyrant Vandal Savage. Rip chooses to steal the Waverider,
a time travel ship, and journey back in time to 2016, where he recruits
Sara Lance, Ray Palmer, Dr. Martin Stein, Jefferson 'Jax' Jackson,
Kendra Saunders, Carter Hall, Leonard Snart, and Mick Rory
to help him stop Savage before he takes over the world.
